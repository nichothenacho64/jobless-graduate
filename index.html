Key

Terminology
FTE = full-time employment
pp = percentage points
Short-term = around 4 months after graduation
Medium-term = around 3 years after graduation

A note on reading the data: Gaps are shown in percentage points, not percentages. For example, a change from 60% to 70% is a 10 pp increase.


Datasets used
QILT (Quality Indicators for Teaching and Learning) – the national survey system for Australian higher education outcomes
  • GOS (Graduate Outcomes Survey) – short-term graduate outcomes
  • GOS-L (Graduate Outcomes Survey - Longitudinal) – medium-term graduate outcomes
ABS (Australian Bureau of Statistics) - Australia's national statistical agency
  • SEW (Survey of Education and Work) – broader education and work context in Australia

Note: Matching cohort data (from GOS-L) is delayed, so recent cohorts do not yet have matching medium-term data
